Hello, my name is Khaya. I am a graduate from Flatiron School's web development boot camp who is seeking out positions in my field. The idea for this website came about after I created the game's functionality with JavaScript. Next, I had to figure out what rock, paper, and scissors images I was going to use when the idea hit me. You know how with animal inspired martial art poses they show the animal in the background? Why not do that with rock, paper, scissors? This thought lead to the site's martial arts game theme.
